Sports

Sports have long been a source of entertainment for people across the world. From the ancient Olympics in Greece with chariot racing to modern day with esports, sports continue to be one the main ways humans connect on an emotional and physical level. Sports give some the chance to grow up to become the next big success story. Sport is made up of competition, tradition, community, and the human spirit. Sports bring together billions of people from around the world and all walks of life Earth to celebrate the games, the athletes and teams, and nations.
